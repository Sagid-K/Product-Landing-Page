Our Story

This everyday shoe is derived from traditional Sudanese men's shoes, named Markoub.  This inspired our very first gender-neutral pair: Signature Maroon.

As a brother-sister partnership, we grew up watching our father and grandfathers wear this wardrobe staple and became even more intrigued when we learned our great-grandmother owned a red pair, despite being culturally recognized as "men's shoes."

It was our way of honoring the ancestral strength of the women and men who paved the way for us to be able to do what we do. One pair led to the next and so it began...
